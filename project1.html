Sirian Overseas UI/UX Case Study

As the UI/UX Developer for Sirian Overseas, I was responsible for designing a website that not only represents the brand's vision but also enhances the user experience. The website facilitates seamless navigation for students aspiring to study abroad, showcasing available services and institutions clearly and effectively.

Project Scope

The scope was to redesign the official website of Sirian Overseas, focusing on improving the user interface and user experience, optimizing it for mobile and desktop devices, and making it more visually engaging while maintaining the brand's identity.

Design Process

I followed a structured design process starting with research, wireframes, and prototypes. The final designs were implemented with a focus on clean layout, easy navigation, and responsiveness.

Challenges & Solutions

The main challenge was creating a user-friendly interface while displaying a large volume of information. By utilizing a minimalistic design approach and ensuring consistent visual hierarchy, the final design achieved clarity without overwhelming the user.
