Yellow Studio UI/UX Case Study

As the UI/UX Developer for Yellow Studio, I worked with an architecture and design studio committed to saving the world through sustainable and innovative design solutions. My focus was on creating a user-friendly digital platform that reflects their commitment to sustainability and creativity.

Project Scope

The project involved developing a user interface that showcases Yellow Studio’s diverse portfolio, while emphasizing their eco-friendly and world-saving design philosophy. The goal was to create a visually engaging platform that effectively communicates their sustainable architectural approach.

Design Process

In this project, I adopted a minimalist and eco-centric design approach, using natural color schemes and modern layouts. User testing played a key role in refining the interface to ensure ease of navigation and clarity of Yellow Studio’s mission and projects.

Challenges & Solutions

Balancing aesthetics with functionality was a significant challenge, as the platform had to highlight Yellow Studio’s creative designs while ensuring an intuitive user experience. Through careful design iteration and feedback, we achieved a harmonious blend of creativity and usability.
